This is a map made for GunGame mod cs 1.6. Therefore there are no guns on the map. We have made an fy_ version (with guns) of the map to and you can find it for download on our webpage.

There are 32 player spawns in the map, but we do not recommend using all of them since it will make it to crowded. We suggest no more than 16 players.

Mapauthors: SadPuppy and Shizuka

Please visit http://addicted2.org/dungeon for maps and downloads.

Idle in #A2 @ Quakenet.

Thanks to all those who have tested the map and given us ideas and inspiration <3

Map objective:

Terrorist kill all Counter Terrorist.

Counter Terrorist kill all Terrorist.
